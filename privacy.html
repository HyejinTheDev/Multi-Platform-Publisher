Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use PostBlast, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you connect a social media platform (YouTube, TikTok), we receive basic profile information such as your display name, email, and profile picture through OAuth authentication.
  • Authentication Tokens: We temporarily store OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens in your browser session to enable video uploads on your behalf.
  • Video Content: Videos you upload are temporarily stored on our server during the upload process and are deleted immediately after being published to the selected platforms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity with connected platforms
  • Upload videos to your selected social media accounts
  • Display your account information within the application

3. Data Storage and Security

Authentication tokens are stored in encrypted server-side sessions and are not permanently saved to any database. Video files are temporarily stored during upload and are automatically deleted after publishing. We do not store your videos permanently.

4. Third-Party Services

PostBlast integrates with the following third-party services:

  • YouTube (Google): Subject to Google Privacy Policy
  • TikTok: Subject to TikTok Privacy Policy

We only request the minimum necessary permissions (scopes) to upload videos on your behalf.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is only shared with the platforms you explicitly choose to connect and publish to.

6. Data Retention

Session data (including tokens) is automatically deleted after 24 hours or when you explicitly disconnect a platform. Video files are deleted immediately after successful upload.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect your social media accounts at any time
  • Request deletion of any data we hold about you
  • Revoke PostBlast's access from your platform account settings

8. Children's Privacy

PostBlast is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
